
Date: August 19

In my search for answers,
I have found remains of what
appears to have been an
advanced civilization in this
remote past.

The discovered artifacts mix
technology with nature, a
surprising and beautiful
combination.

In the midst of the ruins, I feel
like an interloper in the story,
and yet a feeling of deep
connection embraces me.




What was the fate of this
civilization and what knowledge
did they possess?
These questions haunt me
and drive me to keep exploring.

~Ren
